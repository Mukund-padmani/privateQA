## Remote Work Policy

### 1. Purpose
This policy outlines the guidelines for employees who wish to work from home or other remote locations.

### 2. Eligibility
- Full-time employees who have completed their probation period (3 months) are eligible.
- Roles that require physical presence (e.g., security, hardware maintenance) are not eligible.

### 3. Work Hours
- Employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 PM to 4:00 AM EST.
- Total work hours must remain 40 hours per week.

### 4. Equipment
- The company will provide a laptop and a headset.
- Employees are responsible for their own internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps required).
- A one-time stipend of $500 is available for home office setup (monitor, chair, etc.).

### 5. Communication
- Daily stand-up meetings are mandatory at 9:30 AM via Zoom.
- Slack status must be updated to "Away" during breaks.

### 6. Security
- Use of the company VPN is mandatory when accessing internal servers.
- Work devices must not be used for personal tasks.
